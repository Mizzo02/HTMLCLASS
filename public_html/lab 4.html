USS Enterprise

The USS Enterprise was the main ship in a scifi TV series called Star Trek. Here are some general facts about the ship.

Warp Engines

Warp engines were the main propulsion system.

Stardrive Section

The Stardrive section of the ship was where most of the work stations were located.

Saucer Section

The Saucer section was where most of the living space was.

Bridge

The Bridge of the ship was the main control area.

Main Sensor Array

The Main Sensor Array was the ship's primary source of gathering data.
